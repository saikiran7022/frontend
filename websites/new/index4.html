Best bowler

Bowling is one of the main aspects in cricket.In the sport of cricket bowling is the action of propelling the ball toward the wicket defended by a batsman. A player skilled at bowling is called a bowler; a bowler who is also a competent batsman is known as an all-rounder. Bowling the ball is distinguished from throwing the ball by a strictly specified biomechanical definition which restricts the angle of extension of the elbow.The best bowlers depend upon the wickets taken is sorted out and represented.

Best bowling average

A player's bowling average is the number of runs they have conceded per wicket taken. The lower the bowling average is, the better the bowler is performing. It is one of a number of statistics used to compare bowlers, commonly used alongside the economy rate and the strike rate to judge the overall performance of a bowler.I,e. bowling average=runs conceded/wickets taken.The bowlers with highest bowling average is calculated and represented.
